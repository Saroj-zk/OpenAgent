🛡️  Escrow System
🔒
Payment Locked on Purchase
Funds go into a smart contract, never directly to the creator. Held for up to 72 hours while the buyer verifies the agent.
Smart Contract
⚖️
Dispute & Arbitration
If the agent fails, the buyer opens a dispute. Escrow freezes. An arbiter multisig reviews and splits or refunds the payment.
Arbiter Multisig
🛡️
Platform Insurance Pool
A 2.5% platform fee on every transaction funds an insurance pool, protecting buyers against bad actors and failed agents.
2.5% Fee Pool
⏱️
72-Hour Verification Window
Buyers have a full 72-hour window to test the agent before funds release. No more "ship and ghost" by creators.
Time-locked
📉
Creator Strike System
Repeated dispute losses trigger strikes. Multiple strikes slash the listing bond and reduce trust score, blocking new listings.
Listing Bond

The entire flow — lock, verify, dispute, release — is enforced by Solidity contracts on Base. No admin override.
